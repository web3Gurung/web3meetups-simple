Are these Web3 events free or paid?

It varies by event type. Most community meetups and networking events are free to attend. Hackathons typically have free registration but may have application processes. Conferences and multi-day events often have ticket prices, though early-bird discounts are common. Click through to the event page to see specific pricing and registration details. Many events also offer student discounts or scholarships.

How do I find hackathons versus casual meetups?

Check the event titles and descriptions — hackathons usually include keywords like "Hackathon," "Buidl," "Build-A-Thon," or "Ideathon" and span multiple days. Meetups are typically single-day events with titles like "Community Meetup," "Networking Event," or "Info Session." Workshops focus on learning specific skills and often mention technologies like Solidity, smart contracts, or specific blockchains. Conference events usually have "Conference," "Summit," or "Conclave" in the title.

Which cities have the most Web3 activity in India?

Bangalore leads by far with 60%+ of all Web3 events, hosting weekly meetups, monthly hackathons, and major conferences. Mumbai is second, focusing on DeFi, NFTs, and blockchain finance. Delhi NCR hosts government-backed blockchain initiatives and policy conferences. Emerging hubs include Pune (university-driven), Hyderabad (enterprise blockchain), and Chennai (developer communities). Tier-2 cities like Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and Kochi are rapidly growing their Web3 ecosystems.
